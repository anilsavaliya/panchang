About the Panchang
A panchang (five limbs) tracks five daily elements: Tithi (lunar day), Vara (weekday), Nakshatra (ruling star), Yoga (auspice), and Karana (half-period). Together they reveal the quality of each moment — auspicious for new beginnings, caution for ventures, or rest for reflection. This app uses the Drik Panchang calculation system, the most widely used across Gujarat and Maharashtra.
